Questions

Frequently Asked

What is FindMyBaddies?
+
A matchmaking service that rejects swiping. Our multimodal AI builds a personality profile from deep surveys and user-supplied context — chats, screenshots, voice notes — to find exactly one optimal, verified match in 7 days.
How does BaddieGPT work?
+
Upload screenshots of your conversation. BaddieGPT analyzes response intervals, semantic signals, and attachment styles to score interest levels and suggest customized replies in your own voice.
Is my data safe?
+
All uploaded data is encrypted, processed in sandboxed LLM environments, and automatically deleted after match processing. We never store your conversations permanently.
How is the match verified?
+
Every recommendation undergoes identity verification through publicly available social directories — confirming identity, occupation, and baseline interests before delivery.
Where does the matching happen?
+
Everything happens through Telegram and WhatsApp bots. No app to download. Message the bot, answer deep questions, upload context, and wait 7 days. Instagram integration is incoming.
